# Product Naming Options

This product is moving beyond a narrow productivity tool.

The shape now is closer to:
- a life operating system
- a personal chief of staff
- a reflection and execution system
- a private AI-supported dashboard for work, health, wealth, and life direction

The goal of this list is not to decide now. It is to create a serious naming bench for a later full rename pass.

## Naming Criteria

Strong candidates should:
- feel broader than task management
- support work + life + wealth + review + AI
- sound credible, not gimmicky
- be short enough to brand cleanly in a desktop app
- still make sense if the AI layer becomes a major part of the product

## Strongest Shortlist

If narrowing to the most promising candidates now, the strongest five are:

1. **Life OS**
2. **Integral OS**
3. **Northstar**
4. **Helm**
5. **Steward**

## Notes On Tone

### More literal / strategic
- Life OS
- Integral OS
- Northstar OS
- Steward OS

These work best if the product is positioned explicitly as a life operating system.

### More premium / brandable
- Northstar
- Helm
- Steward
- Atlas
- Locus

These work better if you want the product name to feel less generic and more like a standalone company/product brand.

### More reflective / softer
- Cadence
- Harbor
- Horizon
- Mosaic

These are strong if the product leans more toward reflection, rhythm, and whole-life design.

## My Read

The cleanest options for where the product is heading are:

- **Life OS** if you want maximum clarity
- **Integral OS** if you want whole-life scope with a more premium tone
- **Northstar** if you want the strongest brand feel without sounding like generic productivity software
- **Helm** if you want something compact, strong, and directional
- **Steward** if you want the most mature “personal chief of staff for yourself” tone

## Later Rename Pass

When you are ready to rename the product fully, the work should include:

1. App title and window title
2. Top-left brand mark
3. Onboarding copy
4. AI setup copy
5. Docs in `design/`
6. Tauri bundle/app name
7. Any generated assets/icons
8. Search-and-replace of `PPP Flow` and `PPP Flow Desktop`

That should be done as one deliberate rename pass, not piecemeal.
