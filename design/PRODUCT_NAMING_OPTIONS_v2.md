# Product Naming Options — v2 (Independent Ideation)

Generated independently to compare against v1.

## The Brief

The product is outgrowing "PPP Flow". It's becoming a life operating system — daily execution, weekly review, wealth tracking, health coaching, wisdom practices, all tied together with AI. Think: personal chief of staff as software.

## 20 Candidate Names

| # | Name | Angle | Why it could work |
|---|------|-------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Meridian** | The highest point; a guiding line | A meridian is both a peak and a navigational line. Suggests you're orienting your entire life around a central thread. Premium feel, easy to say, very brandable. |

| 6 | **Vantage** | A clear view of the whole picture | Suggests elevation and perspective — seeing your health, wealth, work, and growth from one commanding position. Clean and modern. |
| 7 | **Axis** | The centre around which everything turns | Your days, weeks, finances, health, and practices all orbit a single system. Simple, strong, geometric. |

## Strongest Shortlist

If narrowing to five:

1. **Meridian** — the most naturally brandable; works as app name, company name, and concept
2. **Fulcrum** — the strongest metaphor for what the product actually does (leverage through small daily actions)
3. **Praxis** — the most intellectually honest; theory-into-practice is literally the product's purpose
4. **Vantage** — the cleanest "dashboard for your whole life" name
5. **Arca** — the most distinctive and ownable; short, Latin, memorable

## Tone Groupings

### Architectural / structural
- Fulcrum, Axis, Plinth, Lattice, Trellis

These work if the product positions itself as foundational infrastructure for a well-run life.

### Navigational / directional
- Meridian, Vantage, Beacon, Inflect

These work if the product emphasises seeing clearly and steering deliberately.

### Organic / generative
- Trellis, Taproot, Wellspring, Almanac

These work if the product leans into growth, seasons, and natural rhythm.

### Classical / intellectual
- Praxis, Aegis, Arca, Perihelion

These work if the product wants a more premium, literate, "this is for serious people" tone.

### Energetic / modern
- Verve, Cohere, Mainspring

These work if the product wants to feel alive and kinetic rather than contemplative.

## v1 vs v2 Comparison Notes

v1 leaned toward: nautical metaphors (Helm, Harbor, Compass), explicit OS branding (Life OS, Integral OS), and single-word directional names (Vector, Signal, Horizon).

v2 leans toward: structural metaphors (Fulcrum, Lattice, Plinth), classical/intellectual references (Praxis, Aegis, Arca), and organic growth imagery (Trellis, Taproot, Wellspring).

Between both lists, the candidates worth sitting with longest are probably: **Meridian**, **Northstar**, **Helm**, **Fulcrum**, **Praxis**, and **Arca**. These six have the strongest combination of memorability, brandability, and conceptual fit for where the product is heading.
