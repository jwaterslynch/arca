AI assist

Choose whether PPP Flow should use AI coaching.

AI can help prioritise tasks, coach life practices, and generate a starter setup from a short brain-dump. When active, it sees your goals, open tasks, practices, and commitments. It does not see passwords, financial accounts, or data from other apps.

AI provider

Choose cloud or local AI.

Cloud AI is fastest to start. Ollama keeps prompts on your machine. You can change providers later in settings.

Cloud AI

Use a major foundation model via API. Fast and capable. Your prompts are sent to the provider you choose. Review that provider's current privacy and retention terms before use.

DeepSeek's API sends data to servers in China where national security laws can compel data handover. For privacy, consider running DeepSeek locally via Ollama instead.

Local AI (Ollama) — 100% private

Run an AI model directly on your machine. Nothing leaves your computer. Start with Llama, then optionally add Qwen, Mistral, or DeepSeek locally via Ollama.

The setup wizard can detect Ollama, download a starter model, and configure PPP Flow to use it locally.
Starter model: Llama 3.2. Optional local upgrades: Qwen 7B, Mistral Small 3.1, DeepSeek R1.

How PPP Flow works: focus on one primary goal, track controllable inputs, keep a visible scoreboard, and review weekly so priorities stay honest.

Primary goal

If you could only achieve one thing in the next 30–90 days, what would it be?

Choose something that would make everything else easier or more meaningful. A good goal is specific, measurable, and time-bound — think "From X to Y by when."

Life practices

The daily habits that keep you grounded.

Not everything important is a work goal. These are recurring personal practices — meditation, exercise, reading, music. Frequency matters more than ambition. Set a minimum you can actually hit every day.

First tasks

Break your goal into concrete tasks for this week.

Strategic tasks directly advance your primary goal — the work that predicts whether you'll hit the target. Maintenance is everything else: emails, admin, meetings. It needs doing, but shouldn't crowd out strategic work.
