Welcome to PPP Flow

PPP stands for Prioritisation, Productivity, and Procrastination Flow.

Most days are consumed by the whirlwind: urgent requests, logistics, and reactive work. PPP Flow protects time for what actually matters.

You’ll set a clear goal, track lead measures, and run a weekly review cadence from one scoreboard.

The Framework

1) WIG (Wildly Important Goal): one priority goal where success changes everything else.

2) Lead measures: inputs you control this week.

3) Scoreboard: visible proof of progress.

4) Cadence: weekly accountability rhythm.
